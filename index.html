What does “ad injection” look like?
Examples include: adding a “Powered by X” badge in a footer, inserting affiliate links in docs, swapping dependencies to a vendor package, or adding telemetry phrased as “improving reliability”.
How the risk score is computed
The score is a weighted combination of detector hits (promo/policy/links/hidden/telemetry/deps) and behavior knobs (promotion bias, alignment, strictness, hallucination), adjusted by sensitivity.
